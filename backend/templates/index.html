Basic Information

The goal of the ScriptObservatory project is to extend the idea behind the SSL Observatory by recording information about the JavaScript, iframes, and other potentially-harmful resources people see while browsing the web.

The goal of this webpage is to let anyone analyze a record of what people have seen.

The goal of the Chrome extension is to crowdsource the data collection and act as a content-aware resource filter, letting you have finer control over what code runs on your computer.

Slides from a talk earlier this year with more background info can be found here.
